Pongro Senchey

Pongro Senchey is an informal community situated along a thin strip of land south of the Phnom Penh airport. Families in Pongro Senchey have migrated from rural areas in search of economic opportunity and have been displaced by Phnom Penh’s rapid development. Through workshops led by the Informal Urban Communities Initiative, community members in Pongro Senchey identified infrastructure and skills training as high priorities.

Modular green wall components were developed in response to the community’s desire to make their limited space more beautiful and productive, while also developing skills to contribute to their economic security. Made of heavy duty canvas and recycled materials, each Pongro Plant Pocket features quilted coconut coir backing designed to retain moisture, reducing watering requirements during the dry season and plant establishment. A retractable awning system, flat folding street furniture and grading plans were also created for this project.
